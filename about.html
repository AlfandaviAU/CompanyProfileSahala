About
Sahala Company

 

Sahala Ekspor Indonesia, PT. is a company engaged in international export import on Riau's Province, Indonesia. We export various end-to-end international scale Indonesia coconut, palm, tubers product, and many more.

With the vision of increasing Riau's competitiveness in the world, Sahala Ekspor Indonesia, PT. creates the business ecosystem by reducing many supply chain entities to reach competitiveness and fairness. It is reflected in our slogan that is “We help small business and farmers to reach the global market”

Sahala Ekspor Indonesia Ltd. Is a trading company that imports and exports products from Riau, with Tapioca Flour as its main product. It will be launched in February 2021 based on the company's certificate. Many factors are encouraging founders to set up a company. One of them is Riau’s economic sector, which has not fully recovered after the Covid 19 pandemic struck Indonesia. Riau has the potential to produce exportable produce, but it can't be helped. As an Riau people, the founder wants to maximize exports through Tapioca Flour. Sahala Company is new in Indonesia's import and export agricultural products industry, but our founder has 10 years of online marketing experience both domestically and internationally. For this reason, we are attracting attention from many companies in different countries.
